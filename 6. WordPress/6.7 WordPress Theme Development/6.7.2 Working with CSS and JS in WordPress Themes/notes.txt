What is WP template hierarchy?
A list of all files in custom theme folder.

What function do we use to tell WP where our JS files are located?
wp_enqueue_script()

What WP function do we use in the function.php to tell our theme where our stylesheets are?
wp_enqueue_style()

Where is the best place to load the JS for your theme?
function.php

What should we do with the main css from our static theme?
Copy and paste into our style.css under the comment.

Where is the best place to load your css in a WP theme?
function.php

noconflict
