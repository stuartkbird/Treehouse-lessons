What is WordPress?
	WordPress is a content managment system (CMS)
	Allows for plugins to extend the functionality
	Powers a large portion of the web
	Thousands of themes and plugins available
	Large community around WP with lots of support
	Open source project
	Security and new feature release updates
	Automattic and Matt Mullenweg
