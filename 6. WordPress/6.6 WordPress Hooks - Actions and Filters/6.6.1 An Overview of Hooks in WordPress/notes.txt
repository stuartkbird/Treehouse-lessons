WordPress Hooks - Actions and Filters

Hook
A generic term in WordPress that refers to places where you can add your own code or change what WordPress is doing or outputting by default.
Two types of hooks exist in WordPress, Actions and Filters.

Action
Is a hook that is triggered at a specific time when WordPress is running and lets you take an action.

Filter
Allows you to get and modify WordPress data before it is sent to the database or the browser.
